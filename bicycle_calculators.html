About Trail

Trail, mechanical trail, and wheel flop are distance measurements related to a bike's front end geometry (wheel included) and greatly affect the bike's handling. Exactly how they do so is still unknown, though scientific testing is afoot. That said, French frame builder wisdom suggests that low trail (<= 45 mm) offers good handling when carrying a front load. You can find low trail geometry on randonneur and porteur style bicycles.
